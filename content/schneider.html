Kaiser Wilhelm Institute - Dr. Carl Schneider

Dr. Carl Schneider, Psychiatrist at Heidelberg

Dr. Carl Schneider was arrested for his crimes involving sorting children from the Aktion T4 euthanasia program into death groups. He committed suicide by hanging while awaiting trial for his crimes in 1946.

At Heidelberg University, Schneider conducted studies on psychiatric patients and children. He was part of the group responsible for selecting subjects to be killed, whose brains would then be studied afterward.

Schneider was described as a kind and caring doctor at first. He wished to improve the wellbeing of his mentally deficient patients and encouraged his assistants to spend time with the patients outside of operations. The Nazi regime is said to have corrupted Schneider and repurposed his understanding of patients into cold sorting.
