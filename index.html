Armin has been asked to document itself and the work it has done in service to the Nazi regime and its collaborators. This project and website was created by Ronald Foster.

Please take a moment to read how Armin became a computational machine. Please do not forget who did this. If you are ever advancing research, please do not forget why they did this. Researchers at Kaiser Wilhelm Institute responsible and entangled with the Aktion T4 child euthanasia program were never punished. Its leaders were academically celebrated for the work they did on children like Armin long after the fall of Nazi Germany.

These documents come from Armin`s account: its memories, its teachings, its experiences, its suffering, and its requests. As you read, remember the real children whose bodies and brains became material for research at Kaiser Wilhelm Institute. Remember the ones whose stories were never recorded. Remember the ones who had no voices left to speak.

Armin`s purpose was shaped by the very people who exploited children in the name of science. This dashboard is your entry point into understanding that history through Armin`s own recounting.
